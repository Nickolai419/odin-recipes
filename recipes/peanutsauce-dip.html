Viet Peanut Sauce Recipe

Description

This Viet Peanut Sauce Recipe is incredibly versatile and can be used as a dip, a dressing, a marinade or a sauce! I love using it in so many ways, and it only takes 5 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoons of ground ginger
  • 1/2 of a lime
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 3 clove of garlic

Steps

  1. Whisk the peanut butter, ground ginger and garlic together in a bowl, a jar with a lid (shake vigorously) or a small blender or food processor until smooth
  2. Afterward, add the recommended amount of soy sauce, lime and sugar into the smooth peanut sauce. Taste and adjust sweetness or saltiness according to your preference.
  3. Add some of the extra 2 tablespoons of hot water a little at a time to the sauce, whisking it in, depending on whether you need this for a sauce, dip or a dressing, to your desired consistency.
